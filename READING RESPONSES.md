The Garden Of 
Forking Paths

Jorge Luis Borges, 1941


      ⁃     The story is technically a frame-tale which is pretty interesting. 
      
      ⁃     Historical fiction genre 
      
      ⁃     Spy thriller 
      
      ⁃     It’s explained that the novel and the labyrinth are actually one and the same; taking different forms but being the same construct. 
      
      ⁃     The novel seems “broken” only because in a typical narrative, it’s a character picking an option and moving forward with it.
      
      ⁃     Ts’ui Pên’s novel works by making every imaginable choice at the same time, causing the book to be hard (nearly impossible) to follow.
      
      ⁃     The fact that you can turn the pages and make individual choices without losing access to any other choices you’ve made past present or future is what connects this reading to the concepts discussed in class.
